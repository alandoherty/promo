What is Reach?

Reach is a messaging app built for the web. You'll be able to use Reach wherever you have an internet connection - be it on your phone, tablet, or laptop.

We're about to introduce "social messaging" - and we hope that you love it just as much as we do.

Why Reach?

We all love to keep in contact with one another, and yet the tools that we use to do that are very primitive. They don't account for who youre talking to, or even why - we plan to change that.

How are we doing it?

We're building the Reach API in Node.js, a modern I/O-centric platform that's just perfect for messaging.

Initially, first-party Reach apps will be available for the web and iOS, with Android and Windows Phone following soon after.

Where will Reach be?

Reach can be accessed wherever you have an internet connection and a compatible browser.

Initially, we'll be launching a web app alongside a native iOS app, with more platforms to follow.
